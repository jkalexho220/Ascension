This groundbreaking new Random Map Script unveils a whole new world to play in. This is a custom map that REMEMBERS YOUR PROGRESS. When you open this map in singleplayer, you can choose your class and customize your loadout. When you play this map in multiplayer, you will enter the dungeon and explore randomly generated encounters with your allies! Everything, including your gold, progress, levels, and more, is saved between playthroughs!

Ten years ago, a mysterious tower rose up from the ground in the middle of an uninhabited forest. It reached beyond the clouds to a height no mortal could see. And within this tower were artifacts of ancient power and unimaginable riches.

You are one of many adventurers who have entered the tower seeking power, glory, wealth, or perhaps the thrill of the unknown. No one knows what's at the top of the tower, but there isn't a single person here who isn't aiming for the very top.

What awaits you upon this perilous journey?


There are sixteen classes to choose from, each with their own unique and powerful strengths. Each one can be further customized with the power of relics, which provide a variety of stat boosts and unique passives to elevate your gameplay.

This is not a slow-paced game where you right click everywhere and your numbers get bigger. Unique enemies with custom combat mechanics will attack you at every turn, and mighty bosses with powerful attacks await you at every floor of the tower. You will need to utilize your character's abilities in creative ways to turn the tide of battle in your favor.

Of the sixteen classes, eight of them are showcased below. The rest, you will discover as you play.

An adventurer at heart, the Moonblade brings a little bit of everything to battle.


Passive: Each time you attack, heal all allies for a small percentage of your health.

Special Attack: Summon a wolf companion that fights for you for a short duration.

(Q) Moonbeam: Create a circle of light that silences and damages enemies within for several seconds.

(W) Crescent Strikes: Your next three attacks will stun their targets.

(E) Protection (TOGGLE): Grant all allies invulnerability to damage. This costs 12 favor per second.

An Elven huntress who wields the power of the sun, the Sunbow can heal allies as well as burn enemies with her light magic.


Passive: You can revive allies even when enemies are nearby.

(Q) Sunlight: Create a circle of light that heals allies within for several seconds.

(W) Smiting Rays: Grant an ally laser attacks for a short duration.

(E) Lightwing (TOGGLE): Transform into a Phoenix that deals magic damage. During this time, your heals will damage enemies instead. This costs 6 favor per second.

Born for battle, the Dragon Knight sacrifices his own health to burn away his foes.


Passive: You have bonus lifesteal equal to your percentage of missing health.

Special Attack: Deal damage to yourself and nearby enemies.

(Q) Flaming Impact: Charge towards your cursor for several seconds, dragging enemies with you. At the end of the charge, release an explosion of damage. Launching an enemy into a wall will stun them.

(W) Overheat: Set yourself ablaze, losing health for several seconds. During this time, any damage you take is also inflicted onto nearby enemies.

(E) Inferno (Cost 70): Release a ring of flames that continuously burns enemies and also heals allies.

A master archer whose arrows fall like rain and strike like lightning.


Passive: You deal more damage the further your target is. 10 percent for every 5 meters.

(Q) Stormbolt: Fire an arrow at your cursor that damages and launches the first enemy hit. If they are launched into a wall, they are stunned and damaged a second time.

(W) Disengage: Stun nearby enemies and then teleport a short distance towards your cursor.

(E) Rain of Lightning (Cost 50): For a short duration, your attacks call down lightning strikes that deal damage in an area and ignore armor.

Fast as lightning, the Thunderstepper is never in one spot for more than a second, turning her momentum into a deadly weapon.


Passive: You gain bonus attack by continuously moving but the bonus decays when you stand still.

Special Attack: Your attack chains to nearby enemies, dealing damage to all of them.

(Q) Blitz: Teleport a short distance towards your cursor, damaging and stunning enemies that you pass through. If this is cast while Ride the Lightning is active, this changes your direction of travel instead.

(W) Recharge: Consume all your bonus attack and convert it into healing and favor for yourself.

(E) Ride the Lightning (TOGGLE): Become a fast-moving ball of lightning that bounces off walls. You damage enemies that you touch and your damage increases with every bounce. Costs 8 favor per second.

The Blast Mage is an adept spellcaster specializing in launching a flurry of spells to blow away his foes.


Passive: After you cast a spell, your next attack is empowered. It will explode and create a Star. Stars stay on the ground for a short duration and grant you favor.

(Q) Starfall: A star falls from the sky and deals damage in an area, leaving behind a Star. Enemies struck are stunned.

(W) Magic Missiles: Summon three magic missiles that follow you and lock-on to enemies that get close. Each missile deals damage and creates a Star.

(E) Solar Flare (Cost 50): Fire a laser through each of your Stars, dealing damage to enemies.

A guardian of justice, the Throne Shield offers incredible defensive capabilities for his team.


Passive: When you take damage, reduce it by 2 percent for every 100 health you have.

Special Attack: Stun your target and heal all allies for a percentage of your health.

(Q) Knight's Vow: Choose an ally to be your partner. When they take damage, you take half of it instead. Their attacks contribute to your Special Attack cooldown and their lifesteal heals you. If you cast this ability on your partner again, you will teleport next to them.

(W) Justice: Stun nearby enemies that are not attacking you. Silence the ones that are.

(E) Shield of Light (Cost 60): Absorb all the damage you receive for a short duration. When this ability ends, your next attack fires an enormous laser that deals all the stored damage.

Masterful in chaos, the Spark Witch casts powerful hexes upon her foes.


Passive: Your attack shoots lightning that bounces between enemies, dealing magic damage. Each time you cast a spell, your next attack gains +1 bounce.

(Q) Thundershock: Lightning strikes the ground and deals damage in an area. The cooldown of this ability is reduced for each enemy hit.

(W) Hexbolt: Your next attack is empowered to apply a bonus effect to all enemies hit. This spell has two effects and it switches each time it is cast.
- Polymorph: All enemies hit are temporarily turned into pigs with 0 attack and armor.
- Electrify: Balls of electricity form on each enemy struck, continuously dealing damage in a small area.

(E) Vortex (Cost 80): Create a spacial anomaly that drags enemies into itself and stuns them.
